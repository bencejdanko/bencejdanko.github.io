Inclusive World Portal Industry Co-op

GitHub
A full-stack role-based management portal for the Inclusive World non-profit team.
  • Led a digital transformation for a non-profit, migrating a legacy Google Sheets infrastructure to a centralized PostgreSQL database and Django web application over an 8-month lifecycle, managing over 700 historical volunteers and members records.
  • Directed a cross-functional team of 10 undergraduate developers from SJSU to prototype a React/Supabase MVP, later transitioning to a Django-based architecture to enhance backend stability and scalability.
  • Architected a comprehensive Content Management System (CMS) featuring role-based access control (RBAC), custom dashboards for volunteers, and advanced administrative filtering tools.
  • Engineered complex business logic for course registration, including Stripe API integration, automated attendance tracking, and dynamic reporting.
  • Developed a sophisticated form engine with conditional logic to automate registration requirements and streamline data collection for staff and users.
